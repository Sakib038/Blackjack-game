Blackjack is a popular card game.It is a much complicated game as its rules varies depending on the place.Here a simple version of this game is demostrated.This version of blackjack has some simple and easy rules to follow.Basic rules of this version of the game are as follows.

Rules:

1.When you click/press the 'Hit' button then a random card will be released
2.Each time you click the 'Hit' button a random card will be released every single time.
3.Each card carries a specific point.When 'Hit' button is clicked, points carried by the random card is added with your score.
4.Your total point should be in between 0-21.If it exceeds 21 then your score will show 'Bust'
5.If you think your total points is enough to beat your opponent(bot) after succesive hit such that your total point is greater than opponents point or your point is less than 21 whereas the total points of the opponent exceeds 21 then click the "stand"
6.When you click 'stand' button,you can no more hit and your opponent will release his cards successively.
7.Result:
(i)If your total score is greater than that of your opponent but less than 21,then you win.
(ii)If your score is less than that of your opponent's where both the scores are less than 21,then you lose.
(iii)If both the scores exceed 21 than its a draw.
(iv)If your score is less than 21 but the score of your opponent is greater than 21 then you win and vice versa.
